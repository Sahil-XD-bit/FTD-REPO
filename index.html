header tag:

Represents the top section of a webpage,usually containing the logo,navigation,or title.

main tag:

contains the main content of the webpage, excluding headers ,footers,and sidebars.

footer tag:

represents the bottom section of a webpage ,usually containing copyright ,links or contact info.

section tag:

used to group related content together in a webpage.

article tag :

used for independent content like posts,articles,or user comments.

aside tag:
